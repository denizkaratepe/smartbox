How it works.

Six seamless steps to natural conversation.

1

Capture

Press the button and speak naturally. High-fidelity INMP441 microphone records 3 seconds of audio at 16kHz.

2

Transmit

Wireless transmission over WiFi sends compressed audio data to your local server instantly.

3

Transcribe

OpenAI Whisper converts your speech to text with industry-leading accuracy in multiple languages.

4

Process

OpenAI analyzes your query and generates an intelligent, contextually-aware response.

5

Synthesize

Advanced text-to-speech engine creates natural-sounding voice output at 24kHz.

6

Play

Optimized audio processing delivers clear, distortion-free playback through high-quality amplification.
